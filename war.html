Hacksaw Ridge

2016 • R

DIRECTOR: Mel Gibson

CAST: Andrew Garfield, Sam Worthington, Vince Vaughn

The extraordinary true story of Desmond Doss who, in Okinawa during the bloodiest battle of WWII, saved 75 men without firing or carrying a gun. He was the only American soldier in WWII to fight on the front lines without a weapon, believing that while the war was justified, killing was nevertheless wrong.

Saving Private Ryan

1998 • R

DIRECTOR: Steven Spielberg

CAST: Tom Hanks, Matt Damon, Vin Diesel

Opening with the Allied invasion of Normandy on June 6, 1944, Captain John Miller and his squad are ordered to go behind enemy lines to find Private James Ryan, whose three brothers have been killed in combat. Surrounding the brutal reality of war, the men question the mission: why risk eight lives to save one?

"The Journey That Built Me"

"It is never too late to be what you might have been." — George Eliot

Childhood

1 I, Kimi Bayran, remember playing a lot when I was a kid because those were the happiest times of my life. 2 My favorite cartoon to watch back then was Mr. Bean because it always made me laugh. 3 I also loved watching Cars, especially Lightning McQueen, since he was my favorite character. 4 I liked playing with Legos because it allowed me to build anything I imagined. 5 There was also a time when spider fighting became very popular in our neighborhood. 6 I collected many spiders and felt proud showing them to my cousins. 7 My cousin and I would battle our spiders to see whose was stronger. 8 I would always ask my dad to buy me new spiders so I could train them. 9 My dad helped me take care of them so they would grow strong. 10 I enjoyed feeding them and watching them move around in their small containers. 11 Those moments made my childhood feel exciting and adventurous. 12 When I was in Grade 1, I suddenly became lazy and didn’t want to attend school anymore. 13 I remember pretending to feel sleepy just so I could skip class. 14 My parents would still push me to go because they knew it was important. 15 Even though I resisted, I still learned to enjoy school little by little. 16 I made a few friends who made school more fun for me. 17 I also loved lunchtime because it was when we shared snacks. 18 I enjoyed going home after school because I could watch TV again. 19 Childhood felt simple and carefree, and I wish I could relive some of those moments. 20 Looking back, those experiences shaped a big part of who I am today.

Teenage Years

1 My teenage years have been one of the best chapters of my life. 2 When I was in Grade 7 and 8, I made many memories with my friends. 3 We always played basketball after class because it was our favorite pastime. 4 We laughed, argued, and competed, but those moments brought us closer. 5 Then the pandemic suddenly happened and everything changed. 6 Our normal routine disappeared, and we all had to stay inside our homes. 7 During the lockdown, I gained weight because we could not go out. 8 All we did was play Mobile Legends and Valorant with my friends online. 9 Those games became our way of staying connected despite the distance. 10 It felt strange at first because everything was so uncertain. 11 After several months, the school announced that classes would shift to online learning. 12 I continued attending online classes from Grade 8 until Grade 10. 13 It was challenging because it felt different from traditional school. 14 Sometimes I struggled because the lessons felt harder to learn through a screen. 15 But I also enjoyed being in the comfort of my home. 16 I learned how to manage my time and responsibilities more independently. 17 My teenage years taught me about adapting to unexpected changes. 18 They also reminded me how important friendships are even during hard times. 19 Even though the pandemic was difficult, it became a huge part of my story. 20 Those years will always be memorable because they shaped my growth.

College

1 College truly humbled me and made me see life from a new perspective. 2 There are times when I question myself if this program is really meant for me. 3 Some days feel so overwhelming that I wonder if I chose the right track. 4 The lessons are harder, and the workload is heavier than I expected. 5 I realized that succeeding in college requires patience and discipline. 6 I also learned that time management is crucial if I want to survive every semester. 7 There are moments when the pressure gets too strong, but I continue pushing forward. 8 I met new people who helped me feel less alone in my journey. 9 Some classmates eventually became my close friends. 10 We help each other during difficult subjects and stressful projects. 11 I learned that teamwork is important, especially in group tasks. 12 College also taught me how to be more responsible with my decisions. 13 I experienced sleepless nights because of deadlines and exams. 14 Those nights showed me how determined I can be when I set my mind to something. 15 Even though the challenges are tough, they help me grow into a stronger person. 16 I discovered new things about myself that I didn’t notice before. 17 My college life is not perfect, but it is meaningful. 18 Every struggle I face becomes a lesson that I carry with me. 19 Even with all the doubts, I still believe I can finish what I started. 20 My journey in college continues, and I know it will help shape my future.
